Frequently Asked

Questions Answered.

Can I join if I'm a complete beginner?
Yes — Impact Investments explicitly serves beginners. The educational library, admins available around the clock, and a community culture of helping each new member means you'll never be left behind. Many of Impact's most successful members entered with zero knowledge.
What is CapitalFlow.app?
CapitalFlow.app is Impact's proprietary option flow service — tracking institutional-level order activity in real time. When smart money makes unusual sweeps or blocks in the options market, you see it instantly. Exclusively available to Impact members across all tiers.
Which tier should I start with?
Bronze at $20/month lets you explore Impact risk-free. Gold at $100/month is the recommended starting point for serious traders — full signal access, VIP CapitalFlow, and direct JT interaction. Lifetime at $2,000 is ideal for committed long-term members who want to remove monthly fees entirely.
Is there help available if I get confused?
Yes. Admins are always available to assist. The community culture is one where members actively help each other. Multiple reviews specifically praise how supportive and patient the Impact team is with members at every level — beginner to expert.
Can I benefit if I work full-time?
Yes. Impact explicitly serves busy professionals. The signal service, CapitalFlow alerts, and daily call recordings allow you to benefit even with limited screen time. You don't need to watch the markets all day to profit from Impact's insights.
